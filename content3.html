TV Shows

Strong Girl Bong-Soon

Strong Girl Bong-Soon is a Korean drama revolving around Bong-Soon, a woman with superhuman strength. When An Min-Hyuk, the CEO of a gaming company called Ainsoft hires Bong-Soon as his bodyguard, she must learn how to control her powers in order to protect Min-Hyuk.

My First First Love

My First First Love is a Korean drama that is based around four strangers who have one thing in common: being friends with Yun Tae-o. The drama follows the five friends' journeys as they form new relationships.

The Office

The Office is a sitcom focusing on the employees of Dunder Mifflin, a paper company based in Pennsylvania.

Movies

Spider-Man Far From Home

Spider-Man Far From Home is about Peter Parker and his classmates taking a class trip to Europe. On his trip, he is faced with yet another Avengers-level threat.

500 Days Of Summer

500 Days of Summer is about Tom Hansen, a writer at a greeting-card company, figuring out his priorities when his relationship with his girlfriend, Summer, ends abruptly.

The Perks of Being a Wallflower

The Perks of Being a Wallflower is based off of a book of the same name. It follows high-school freshman Charlie Kelmeckis as he goes through some major changes in his life.
