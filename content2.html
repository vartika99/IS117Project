Strong Girl Bong-Soon

Strong Girl Bong-Soon is a Korean drama revolving around Bong-Soon, a woman with superhuman strength. When An Min-Hyuk, the CEO of a gaming company called Ainsoft hires Bong-Soon as his bodyguard, she must learn how to control her powers in order to protect Min-Hyuk.

The Office

The Office is a sitcom focusing on the employees of Dunder Mifflin, a paper company based in Pennsylvania.

New Girl

New Girl is about three best friends and housemates, Nick, Schmidt, and Winston, who have to learn how to get used to their new housemate, Jess.

The Flash

The Flash is based off of the DC Comics character of the same name. It follows Barry Allen as he fights crime to protect his family, friends, and the citizens of Central City.

Good Trouble

Good Trouble is a spin-off of The Fosters, and it circles around sisters and roommates, Callie and Mariana, as they navigate life after college.

The Good Place

The Good Place follows four strangers who are trying to navigate life after death.
